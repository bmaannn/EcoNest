About Us

Welcome to EcoNest, where our journey began with two friends, Andrew Alia and Basith Salami, who combined their love for technology and botany to revolutionize indoor gardening. Our mission at EcoNest is to empower indoor gardeners by providing unparalleled control and visibility over their plant environments. With our flagship system, Hummingbird, we offer a sophisticated, user-friendly experience complete with universal hardware compatibility and real-time monitoring. This system is a testament to our commitment to innovation, delivering smart alerts and automation that simplify gardening. Our team, led by co-founders Andrew Alia, Basith Salami, Mukhtar Abbas, and Roland Njoya, is driven by a shared vision of integrating IoT technology with horticulture, ensuring that every home can experience growth and sustainability.
